Parametric Diagram

Element Type: Stereotype


Description: A parametric diagram describes the parametric relationships among the properties associated with blocks, parts, parametric constraints, parametric constraints usages and items that flow between them. The parametric diagram is used to integrate behavior and structure models with engineering analysis models such as performance, reliability, and mass property models.
